The Platform

A clinical approach to team energy.

Move beyond superficial wellness perks. Provide scientific preventative care, cognitive fitness tools, and real clinical access that fits seamlessly into the modern product workflow.

Asynchronous Check-ins

Bi-weekly micro-assessments reviewed by licensed practitioners to identify early signs of burnout or acute stress before they impact performance.

  • Evidence-based GAD-7 & PHQ-9
  • Slack & Microsoft Teams automated delivery

Anonymized Analytics

Equip HR and leadership with team-level insights without compromising individual privacy. Track organizational health scores across departments.

  • Department-level heatmaps & trending
  • Strictly enforced privacy firewalls (cohorts n>5)

Board-Certified Network

Instant, frictionless access to vetted therapists, coaches, and care navigators. We maintain a high-quality, specialized network for modern professionals.

  • Guaranteed matching within 48 hours
  • 1-on-1 video or secure text-based therapy

Personalized Care Plans

Employees receive actionable, private care plans tailored to their specific assessment results, algorithmically triaged for severity by clinical protocols.

  • Stepped-care model (Self-guided to Clinical)
  • Continuous longitudinal tracking
