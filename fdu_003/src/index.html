Our Terroir

Beyond the
Surface of the Cup.

We believe tea is an agricultural legacy, not a commodity. We visit every estate, from the misty peaks of Alishan to the ancient forests of Yunnan, ensuring the preservation of biodiversity and the dignity of the farmer.

Our direct-trade model provides 3x the local market rate to our partners, allowing for the continued craftsmanship of wild-harvested batches that would otherwise be lost to industrialization.
