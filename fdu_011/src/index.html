Common Inquiries

A traditional consultant delivers a point-in-time report and leaves. A vCISO (Virtual Chief Information Security Officer) acts as an integrated part of your leadership team, owning the ongoing strategy, presenting to your board, and continuously adapting your posture as your business scales.

We focus on structural architecture and strategic advisory. While we do not perform offensive penetration testing internally, we manage the scoping, vendor selection, and remediation planning for third-party pentests to ensure you receive actionable value rather than just an automated scan report.

Our typical cloud architecture reviews span 3 to 4 weeks. This includes read-only access provisioning, automated baseline scans, manual architecture review, and the presentation of a prioritized remediation roadmap to engineering leadership.
