Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a photography technical course?
No. We do not teach lighting, camera settings, or retouching. FrameSchool assumes you already have a body of work. We teach the curation, editing, sequencing, and presentation of that work.
How much time does the cohort require?
Expect to commit 4-6 hours per week. This includes watching the modules, completing the sequencing assignments, and attending or watching replays of the live critique sessions.
Can I upgrade from Self-Paced to the Cohort later?
Yes. If you start with the Self-Paced tier, you can apply the amount paid toward the next available Cohort tuition, subject to seat availability.
