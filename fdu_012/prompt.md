# fdu_012

## Round 1
Create a premium long-scroll single-file `src/index.html` for a **corporate website** called **SlateLedger**.
Concept: a fintech invoicing product website for founders who want cash-flow clarity, cleaner collections, and modern finance operations without old-school ERP heaviness.
Audience: design-conscious founders, finance leads, and operators who want premium fintech trust signals with sharp product presentation.
Style direction: **Dark Mode** with references to modern fintech product pages, premium finance dashboards, and Awwwards-inspired dark UI storytelling.
Component language: layered dark cards, contrast-rich stat panels, tabbed product modules, elevated CTA blocks, and polished case-study sections.
Color direction: near-black, graphite, deep indigo, cool steel gray, and controlled electric accents used sparingly.
Typography direction: sharp modern headings with highly readable body text and crisp numeric treatments.
Motion direction: controlled, technical, premium, and precise rather than flashy.
Build a complete page with these sections: navbar, hero, credibility strip, invoice automation modules, cash-flow insights, process walkthrough, case studies, metrics, testimonials, FAQ, CTA, footer.
Required interactions: hover states, tabs, accordion, scroll reveal, smooth scrolling.
The page should feel like a polished modern fintech brand site rather than a dark generic dashboard mockup.

## Round 2
Deepen the layout and content density.
The hero should combine strong fintech clarity, a dark premium UI preview, and a concise value proposition around faster collections and better forecasting.
Add believable copy around invoice automation, approval rules, payment reminders, receivables forecasting, finance collaboration, and global billing readiness.
Balance trust-building with conversion through clear services, process explanation, case-study framing, and premium credibility cues.
Use richer module composition so the product story feels specific and financially credible rather than abstract.
Keep enough vertical depth for a long full-page screenshot, with multiple strong section transitions and no thin filler sections.

## Round 3
Polish all interaction states and compliance details.
Tabs should feel crisp and clearly stateful, accordions should expand smoothly with strong contrast, and scroll reveal motion should guide attention without slowing the page down.
Smooth scrolling should make anchor navigation feel premium rather than ornamental.
Ensure the layout is responsive for desktop and tablet widths while preserving the sharp dark fintech identity.
Keep all CSS and JS inline inside `index.html`.
Do not use React, Vue, Svelte, jQuery, or any build step.
Do not reference local images, local fonts, local CSS, or local JS.
Use remote imagery only if needed and inline SVG icons.
Return only the final `src/index.html` content with no explanation.

## Round 4
Final refinement pass.
The result should feel technical, premium, and financially credible while still being easy to scan and convert from.
Avoid crypto aesthetics, noisy neon overload, and compliance-sounding filler without product depth.
Ensure enough depth for a long full-page screenshot and visible interactive states throughout.
Return one complete self-contained `index.html`.
