# fdu_019

## Round 1
Create a premium long-scroll single-file `src/index.html` for an **education platform** called **Signal Academy**.
Concept: an AI observability education platform for mid-market product teams that need practical training on evaluation, tracing, incident review, and production model quality.
Audience: mid-market product teams, ML leads, and operators who want a calm serious learning environment around production AI reliability.
Style direction: **Minimalist** with references to editorial restraint, premium monochrome product sites, and calm modern learning interfaces.
Component language: minimal cards, quiet curriculum modules, understated navigation, refined comparison sections, and elegant CTA framing.
Color direction: warm neutrals, charcoal, soft stone, muted olive accents, and disciplined contrast with low visual noise.
Typography direction: refined minimalist headings paired with restrained sans-serif body text.
Motion direction: calm, subtle, tactile, and understated.
Build a complete page with these sections: navbar, hero, learning path overview, curriculum tracks, labs and simulations, instructor highlights, team outcomes, pricing, testimonials, FAQ, CTA, footer.
Required interactions: hover states, pricing toggle, modal CTA, smooth scrolling, navbar blur.
The page should feel like a real professional academy rather than a course marketplace or AI hype page.

## Round 2
Deepen the layout and content density.
The hero should be calm and authoritative, showing that the platform teaches operational AI reliability without hype or academic fluff.
Add believable copy around evaluation design, regression detection, incident review, prompt governance, monitoring workflows, and cross-functional training outcomes.
Show how learners move from discovery to enrollment to guided progress and measurable outcomes with enough concrete detail to feel like a real program.
Keep the page visually restrained but content-rich, with real educational depth rather than glossy abstraction.
Keep enough vertical depth for a long full-page screenshot, with multiple strong section transitions and no thin filler sections.

## Round 3
Polish all interaction states and compliance details.
Make the pricing toggle visibly change plan framing or team-training packaging, and ensure the modal CTA opens and closes cleanly without feeling intrusive.
Smooth scrolling and the navbar blur transition should feel polished and intentionally branded.
Ensure the layout is responsive for desktop and tablet widths while preserving the calm serious tone.
Keep all CSS and JS inline inside `index.html`.
Do not use React, Vue, Svelte, jQuery, or any build step.
Do not reference local images, local fonts, local CSS, or local JS.
Use remote imagery only if needed and inline SVG icons.
Return only the final `src/index.html` content with no explanation.

## Round 4
Final refinement pass.
The result should feel professional, calm, and content-rich while still making enrollment and team adoption feel straightforward.
