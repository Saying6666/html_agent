# fdu_028

## Round 1
Create a premium long-scroll single-file `src/index.html` for an **event or conference page** called **Dark Circuit Summit**.
Concept: a robotics education academy launching a serious dark-mode summit for developers and operators who want hardware demos, curriculum innovation, and applied automation dialogue.
Audience: developers and operators, robotics leads, technical educators, and systems-minded attendees who want depth over hype.
Style direction: **Dark Mode** with references to serious applied-tech summits, premium developer events, and cinematic dark product storytelling.
Component language: layered dark cards, contrast-rich speaker modules, technical agenda panels, elevated ticket sections, and sharp CTA blocks.
Color direction: near-black, graphite, deep indigo, cool steel gray, and carefully used electric accents.
Typography direction: sharp modern headings with highly readable body text and strong technical hierarchy.
Motion direction: controlled, technical, premium, and precise rather than flashy.
Build a complete page with these sections: sticky navbar, hero, keynote speakers, agenda, hardware demo tracks, workshop labs, sponsor strip, ticket tiers, FAQ, footer.
Required interactions: hover states, tabs, accordion, scroll reveal, smooth scrolling.
The page should feel like a serious applied-tech summit rather than a generic developer conference dark theme.
