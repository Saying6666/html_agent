The Menu

A Sequence of Chapters

Rather than disconnected dishes, Ember Table presents an unfolding narrative. Each chapter pays homage to a specific elevation, climate, and technique.

Prologue

Smoke & Sea

Cold-smoked local bivalves, kelp oil, and a delicate spruce broth. An awakening of the palate referencing the mist-heavy mornings of the coastline.

Act I

Forest Floor

Foraged chanterelles, lacto-fermented root vegetables, and pine-needle ash. An earthy homage to the damp, fertile soil of the old-growth woods.

Act II

The Coals

Dry-aged venison suspended over glowing embers of cherrywood, paired with preserved summer berries and reduced bone essence.

Epilogue

Ash & Orchard

Burnt honey ice cream, caramelized late-harvest apples, and a dusting of activated charcoal. A lingering, melancholic sweetness.

Details & Protocol

Guest Notes

How does the reservation cadence work?

We release tables for the following month on the 1st of every month at 10:00 AM local time. Due to our strictly limited seating of 14 guests per evening, reservations often fill within hours. We recommend utilizing our waitlist feature for sudden cancellations.

Can dietary restrictions be accommodated?

Because our narrative is strictly tied to specific techniques and micro-seasonal ingredients, we cannot accommodate severe aversions or vegan diets. We can, however, adjust for pescatarian and gluten-free diets with a minimum of 48 hours' notice prior to your reservation.

What is the expected attire?

We request smart, elegant attire that reflects the elevated nature of the evening. Jackets are preferred for gentlemen, but not strictly required. Please refrain from wearing activewear, shorts, or open-toed shoes.

What is the pacing of the tasting experience?

The 12-course sequence is designed to unfold over approximately two and a half to three hours. It is an immersive, leisurely experience meant to be savored without haste.
