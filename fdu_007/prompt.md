## Round 1

Concept: a robotics education academy presented as a vibrant editorial publication that explains programs, student stories, labs, and future-facing curriculum.
Audience: mid-market product teams, innovation leads, and parents or operators evaluating a modern robotics learning brand with strong visual polish.
Style direction: **Gradient Mesh** with references to futuristic product launches, premium learning startups, and immersive editorial hero systems.
Component language: glow-backed cards, layered feature panels, luminous stat chips, polished content rails, and modular article highlights.
Color direction: deep plum, electric blue, vivid violet, cyan, and restrained coral accents blended through premium gradient transitions.
Typography direction: bold display-driven headings with smooth rounded sans-serif support text.
Motion direction: fluid, expressive, high-energy, and polished without becoming chaotic.
Build a complete page with these sections: navbar, hero feature story, academy overview, featured programs grid, lab highlights, student outcomes, editorial resource rail, enrollment CTA, FAQ, footer.
